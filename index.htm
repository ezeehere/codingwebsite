We're a student-led community passionate about technology, coding, and innovation.
Whether you're into building apps, designing websites, exploring AI
or just starting your coding journey — this club is for you.

As a core member, you'll get the chance to:

• Collaborate with like-minded peers • Organize coding events, workshops, and hackathons • Contribute to real-world tech projects and our club's growth
We're not just about writing code — we're about building a culture of learning, creativity, and impact.
If you're enthusiastic, curious, and ready to lead — this is your place.

🧠Core Leads

Lead and Co Lead Set vision, approve projects, guide the team. Organize meetings, coordinate with faculty.

🌐Web Development Lead

Leads website and web-based projects. Works with frontend/backend contributors. Teaches others via sessions or paired coding.

📱App Development Lead

Builds mobile apps (Flutter / React Native etc.). Guides members interested in app development. Can start Android-only at first, grow later.

🎨Design Lead

Heads UI/UX design for all projects. Uses Figma/Canva, creates user flows, etc. Ensures visual consistency and accessibility.

📂Project Coordinator

Keeps track of all active projects. Maintains task lists, GitHub issues, Notion docs. Ensures members stay unblocked.

🤝Events Head.

Organizes workshops, collabs, and online events. Connects with other clubs, colleges, alumni. Handles Google Forms, registrations, Discord/Telegram moderation
